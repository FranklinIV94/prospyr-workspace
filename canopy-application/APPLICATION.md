### 2. What Are You Building?
**PDS (Proven Data System) — Trust Infrastructure for AI Agents**

AI agents are becoming autonomous economic actors, but there's no trust layer. Who do you trust? How do they prove identity? How do you verify reputation?

PDS is an onchain trust registry that:
- Registers AI agents with verifiable onchain identity (ERC-8004 compatible)
- Tracks reputation through interaction history
- Generates cryptographic attestations for trust verification
- Enables delegation and cooperation without middlemen
