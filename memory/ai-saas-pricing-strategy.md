# AI SaaS Pricing Strategy - March 2026

**Source:** Vectal founder (David) - $155K ARR journey
**Date:** March 9, 2026

## Core Insight
The fastest way to make more money is NOT more features, traffic, or marketing — it's fixing pricing.

## Key Principles

### The Math Problem
- **$20/mo:** Need 1,000 clients for $20K/mo
- **$300/mo:** Need 67 clients for $20K/mo
- **$3K setup + $300/mo:** Only 7 new clients/month

### Anchor Pricing
- Add expensive tier (even "Coming Soon") to make main offer look cheap
- Default to annual view on pricing page
- Example: $100/mo anchor makes $20/mo Pro look like a steal

### Annual Plans = Paid Acquisition Fuel
- $800/year (vs $1,200) = 4 months free
- Single annual customer = can spend $700 on ads and still profit day one
- Monthly $20 plans cannot support paid acquisition profitably

### Upsell Existing Customers
- Grandfather old pricing (keeps them happy)
- Offer upgrades at preferential rate
- "Negative churn" = upgrades offset lost customers
- Simplest path to revenue growth

### Stop Overthinking Costs
- Different limits for different tiers
- Free = cheap models; Paid = premium
- Don't solve problems you don't have yet
- API costs = problem of success, not failure

## ALBS Application
1. Position as niche specialist, not generalist
2. Annual prepay for all retained clients
3. Setup fees + monthly (not just hourly)
4. Upsell current book of business
5. Anchor with "enterprise" tier even if not sold
